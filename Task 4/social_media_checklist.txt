Social Media Do’s
1) Keep it professional: Make sure your LinkedIn profile stays updated with your latest ECE projects or certifications.
2) Double-check privacy: Regularly review your account settings to control who can actually see your personal posts.
3) Use 2FA: Always enable two-factor authentication on your main accounts—it’s the easiest way to prevent a hack.
4) Give credit: If you’re sharing someone else's design or code, always mention the original creator.
5) Think before you post: Take five seconds to consider how a future employer might view a public comment or photo.

Social Media Don’ts
1) Don't share secrets: Never post private college details, exam papers, or confidential project info.
2) Avoid the drama: Don’t get pulled into heated or unprofessional arguments in public comment sections.
3) Don't trust every link: Avoid clicking on or sharing suspicious links, even if they look like they’re from friends.
4) Don't tag without asking: Respect other people's privacy and don't tag them in photos or locations without a quick check first.
5) Don't reuse passwords: Avoid using the same simple password for your social media and your college accounts
