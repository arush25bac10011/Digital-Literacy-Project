1) The PIN Rule: Never enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code if someone claims they are sending you money.
2) Double-Check IDs: Always verify the UPI ID (VPA) before approving any transaction, especially if the requester is not in your contacts.
3) App Privacy: Set a separate screen lock or biometric lock for your payment apps to prevent unauthorized access if your phone is stolen.
4) Link Caution: Don't click on "lottery" or "job offer" links sent via WhatsApp or SMS, as these are often phishing attempts.
5) Update Regularly: Keep your banking apps and phone OS updated to ensure you have the latest security patches.
6) Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into bank accounts or making payments while connected to public college or cafe Wi-Fi.
7) Social Engineering: Be wary of callers who create a sense of extreme urgency or panic regarding your bank account.
8) Check Statements: Review your transaction history at least once a week to spot any small, unauthorized deductions early.
